Features

  • SIP User Management: Effortlessly create and manage SIP extensions.
  • WebRTC Odoo Phone: Seamlessly make and receive calls directly in Odoo!
  • Call Flow Automation: Design Twilio or Elevenlabs powered IVR flows directly in Odoo.
  • Real-Time Call Analytics: Track calls with detailed statistics, all within Odoo.
  • Call Recording: Ensure call quality, build a robust knowledge base, and meet compliance requirements with secure and seamless call recording.
  • CRM / Helpdesk Integration: Automatically link call records to customer data.
  • AI-Powered Features: Leverage AI-driven call summaries and transcriptions to save time and improve efficiency (demo).
  • Automatic dialing out to deliver individual voice messages or connect to agents.
  • Odoo CE, EE and odoo.sh are supported.
  • Click to call from Partner / Lead / Tickets form.
  • Partners calls history with consolidation on parent company with grouping by partner employees.
  • Automatic opening of partner / lead / ticket form on call.
  • Normalize your phone numbers database (check if number is valid before save).
  • Partner creation from active call or call history.
  • Advanced call history analytics.
  • SMS / Whatsup messaging.
  • Voice AI Bot integration.
  • Custom development available for request.
